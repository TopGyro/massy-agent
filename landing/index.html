// 01 — Capabilities

Seven Specialised Tools

The agent autonomously chains these tools based on your query — no manual routing. Each tool reads live data from AWS DynamoDB.

01
Inventory Status
Real-time stock levels and reorder alerts across all 10 Caribbean locations.
DynamoDB · days-of-supply · reorder flags
02
Demand Forecast
Market basket analysis and next-item chain prediction over 60 days of order history.
FP-Growth · Markov chain · association rules
03
Warehouse Optimiser
Crane pick sequencing to maximise throughput within weight capacity constraints.
0-1 Knapsack · dynamic programming
04
Route Planner
Optimal delivery routing across the Caribbean — handles road and inter-island sea legs.
VRP · nearest-neighbour · 2-opt improvement
05
Sustainability
Carbon footprint and fuel cost per operation, with sea vs road counterfactual.
IPCC AR6 factors · TTD cost model
06
Network Graph Analysis
Identifies single points of failure and product co-purchase clusters in the network.
NetworkX · betweenness centrality · community detection
07
Shipping Compliance
HS code classification, CARICOM duty eligibility, and full customs declarations.
HS codes · CARICOM CET · VAT calculation
